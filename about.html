About us
Creators: Victoria DeGrezia and Gabriella Magras
We started this company freshman year of college and fell in love with the art and creativity of designing shirts. We both grew up in Staten Island, New York and are currently seniors in college. It started off as us just having fun and making shirts for our family and close friends, but it unexpectedly turned into something more. Our designs are one of a kind and we hope to make your imagination come to life.
